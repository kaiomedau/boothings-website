About Us

We believe that NFTs are the future, far beyond profile pictures. However, all evolution must start from a place and with it comes empirical evolution. By doing, we learn, by living, we evolve.

Boo Things is a project to open the doors of the NFT universe to more people. We love and believe in the experience that this community is capable of promoting, so we believe that everyone should live and learn with this experience, no matter the budget.

Here you will have access to a free and complete experience, from a whitelist slot to the reveal of your NFT, and still receive a very carefully made Ghost art for your collection.

FAQ'S

Price & Mint Amount
+

Ghosts are free! However, to ensure that as many people as possible can participate free of charge, we created an anti-greed and anti-bots system, in which, from the second ghost onwards, the price starts at 20 MATIC and doubles with each new mint.

Why a whitelist if ghosts are free?
+

This is a way to generate awareness and engagement, as each place on the list guarantees a ghost. In addition, it is another layer of security against bots. Our goal is to have as many people as possible on our list, ensuring that more real people have the opportunity to join our community.

How can I get a whitelist slot?
+

Participating in our community. We have events, missions and giveaways all the time, just participate. Remember that the idea is to have all NFTs on the listed, so there will be no shortage of opportunities for you to win your spot.

When Will Boo Things Launch?
+

We are still deciding on the final release date. But the more engagement we get, the faster the launch will arrive.
The launch will take place in two phases. The first 24 hours will be dedicated to the whitelist mint. We will then release it to the general public.

Do You Have a Roadmap?
